Everything in the Universe Is Code!

This is my world view, and the foundation for my view is built upon countless, proven, scientific theorums and a great amount of physical evidence. Take the Fibonacci Sequence for an example. This pattern can be seen in flowers and seashells, amoung many others in the natural universe. Look at a DNA strand, and you will see another pattern. The patterns in nature are abundant. It is the job of scientists to figure out the code behind the patterns. Now, in the digital age, we have discovered that everything can be reduced to a series of 0s and 1s. Computers show us the greatest proof that everything is code. This discovery is why I am continually striving to understand complex problems as a developer--to understand the code.
